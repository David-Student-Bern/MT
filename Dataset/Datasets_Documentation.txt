# Content
These two dataset contain data from other catalogs (same in both datasets):
    - OMNI - solar wind magnetic field and plasma data shifted to the Earth's bow shock nose (BSN)
    - LASP - CLS Solar Radio Flux 

and information unique to either GRACE-FO 1 (GFOC) or SWARM-A (SWMA):
    - BERNESE - Satelite position, velocity and atmospheric density
    - SPACETRACK - Semimajor axis from TLE data
    - STL - "Seasonal and Trend decomposition using Loess"
    - DECAY RATES - orbital decay rate in m/day & slope, intercept, residual, standard error and deviation
    - GENERAL - time and Day of Year
    - MANEUVER FLAGS â€“ flags for maneuver and missing days
    - OTHER NOTES

for more detailed information about these categories see Dataset/Dataset_MSc/data_doc_all.json.
